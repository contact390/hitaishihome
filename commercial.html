Frequently Asked Questions

Commercial leases typically range from 3-10 years, with 5 years being most common for office and retail spaces. Industrial properties often have longer terms of 7-10 years. We can negotiate flexible terms based on your business needs.

Our specialists conduct a thorough needs assessment considering your business type, customer demographics, employee needs, growth projections, and budget. We then match you with properties that meet your operational requirements and strategic goals.

Commercial property ownership typically includes property taxes (1-4% of value annually), insurance (0.5-1.5%), maintenance (1-2%), potential common area maintenance (CAM) fees, and possibly property management fees (4-10% of rents). We provide detailed cost projections for each property.

Commercial transactions involve more complex due diligence (zoning, environmental assessments, lease reviews), longer closing periods (60-90 days typically), more negotiation points (tenant improvement allowances, lease terms), and different financing structures (lower LTV ratios, shorter loan terms).

Returns vary by property type and location, but historically: Office spaces yield 6-10% ROI, retail properties 7-12%, and industrial properties 8-14%. Combined with average annual appreciation of 3-6%, total returns typically range from 9-20% annually. Use our ROI calculator for property-specific projections.
