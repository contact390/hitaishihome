Retail Space FAQs

Answers to common questions about our retail properties

Our retail leases typically include the base rental space and common area maintenance (CAM) charges. Many locations include utilities, and some offer build-out allowances for tenant improvements. Specific inclusions vary by property and lease type (gross lease vs. net lease). Our leasing team will provide complete details for each property you're considering.

We use advanced people counting technology at our properties, including infrared sensors and video analytics, to accurately measure daily, weekly, and seasonal foot traffic patterns. For mall locations, we provide verified visitor statistics from the property management. All our traffic data is available to prospective tenants to help with their location decisions.

We offer flexible lease terms to suit different business needs: short-term 3-6 month pop-up leases, 1-2 year medium-term leases, and traditional 3-5 year leases with renewal options. Some properties offer percentage leases where rent is based partly on sales performance. Our leasing specialists can help determine the best structure for your business model.

Most of our spaces can be customized to suit your brand. We offer tenant improvement allowances on many properties (typically $10-$50 per sq ft depending on location and lease term). We have preferred contractors who understand our buildings' requirements for efficient build-outs. All modifications require approval to ensure they meet building codes and property standards.

Start by contacting our retail leasing team to discuss your needs. After identifying suitable properties, we'll guide you through our application process which includes: business documentation (license, references), financial statements or business plan, personal financial information for guarantors, and application fee. Approval typically takes 3-5 business days after complete submission.
