Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the office rental?

All Hitiashi office rentals include high-speed internet, utilities, cleaning services, access to shared amenities (like pantries and lounges), and basic maintenance. Private offices also include furniture and meeting room access. See specific listings for complete details.

Can I customize my office space?

Private offices allow limited customization like artwork and branding elements. Custom buildouts offer complete design flexibility including wall configurations, flooring, lighting, and branded environments. Our design team will work with you to create your ideal workspace.

What are the lease terms?

We offer flexible terms to match your needs: coworking spaces are month-to-month, private offices have 3-month minimums with discounts for longer commitments, and custom spaces typically require 1-3 year leases depending on buildout requirements.

How does parking work?

All locations offer parking options. Coworking members can purchase daily parking passes or monthly permits. Private offices include 1-2 dedicated spots depending on size, with additional spots available. Custom spaces include negotiated parking allocations. EV charging is available at all locations.
