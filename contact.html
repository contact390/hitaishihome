Frequently Asked Questions - Hitaishi Homes

How do I cancel my booking with Hitaishi Homes?

To cancel your booking, please log into your Hitaishi Homes account, navigate to your bookings, and select 'Cancel Booking'. If you need assistance, contact our support team.

When will I receive possession of the property?

Possession timelines vary by project. Typically, possession is granted within 30–90 days after all documentation is complete and payment milestones are met. Check your booking details or contact our team for specifics.

Where can I pay rent or make payments?

All payments, including rent, can be made securely through the Hitaishi Homes online portal. We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking for your convenience.

How can I contact interested buyers or tenants?

You can reach out to interested parties directly through our secure in-platform messaging feature or via the contact details shared with your listing (with your permission).

Why isn't my property listing showing up?

Please ensure that your listing is complete, meets our quality guidelines, and has been approved by our moderation team. It may take up to 24 hours for listings to go live.

How can I promote my listing for better visibility?

Boost your listing by selecting the 'Promote' option. You can choose from featured packages to get better visibility on our homepage, search results, and email alerts.
